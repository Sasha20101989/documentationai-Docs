## Getting Started

To begin organizing your project docs:

1. Sign in at `https://felugin.com`
2. Click **Create New Project**
3. Enter your project name
4. Add your first documentation page
5. Save and share links with your team

## Tips

- Use the sidebar to structure docs by topic
- Invite collaborators from your dashboard
- Access version history from the top bar
