title: Can't Access Documentation Space
description: Quick steps for access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Login fails or shows error
- Docs/dashboard not loading

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct. Click "Forgot password?" to reset if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache and cookies">
    Try reloading after clearing your browser's cache and cookies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, email `support@felugin.com`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
